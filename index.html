Timeline

This is a timeline visualization tool built with the vis.js library. You can view up to 3 timelines simultaneously, and discover which events were happening around the world at different times in human history.

Memento Mori

HTML | CSS | JavaScript

A simple counter, showing you how much time you probably have left until you die. Just put in your birthday and the number of years you expect to live. Meant to put things into perspective and motivate you to do something good with your time.

Go to website

Sub Cover

Python | Tkinter

A window that covers hard subtitles in videos, and can also be used to display other subtitle files. Useful for immersion when learning a language. Font, colors and position can be customized.
